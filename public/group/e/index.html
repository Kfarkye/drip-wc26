Analysis

Germany desperately seeks redemption after back-to-back group stage exits in 2018 and 2022 — a historically unprecedented collapse for a four-time champion. Wirtz and Musiala represent the most exciting young attacking midfield duo in the tournament. Ecuador provides legitimate South American quality. Ivory Coast, the reigning AFCON champions, are dangerous but underpriced. Curaçao makes a historic World Cup debut as the smallest nation in the field with a population of approximately 150,000.
